A. C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada IAST: Abhaya Caraṇāravinda Bhaktivedānta Svāmī Prabhupāda; (1 September 1896 – 14 November 1977) was a spiritual, philosophical, and religious teacher from India who spread the Hare Krishna mantra and the teachings of "Krishna consciousness" to the world. Born as Abhay Charan De and later legally named Abhay Charanaravinda Bhaktivedanta Swami.
